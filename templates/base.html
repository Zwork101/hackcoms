Mission

Our mission is to connect students
with similar ideas and goals
inorder to create projects which
would otherwise be impossible.
We believe that when people come
together, they can do incredible
things. To that end we at GroupUs
developed this website inorder to
connect individuals and groups,
encourage teamwork, and to
create a stonger community.
We believe in inclusivity and as such
have taken steps to ensure that
our serices are available to all.
